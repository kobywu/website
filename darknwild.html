Dark & Wild
About the Albums
Dark & Wild is the first studio album BTS released on August 19, 2014. It includes 14 tracks with "Danger" as the main track. "War of Hormone" and "Danger" charted on the 2014 Billboard World Digital Songs. The album peaked at number 3 on the Gaon Monthly Chart and the World Albums and number 27 on Top Heatseekers. It also won the Album Divison at the 29th Golden Disk Awards in 2015.

Tour
This album was promoted during the first half of the BTS Live Trilogy Episode 2: The Red Bullet from October 17 to December 20. It was an Asian Tour going to only 5 countries of Japan, Philippines, Thailand, South Korea and Singapore.
