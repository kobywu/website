About the Albums
The Most Beautiful Moment in Life is a 3 part series containing the albums "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Part 1", "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Part 2" and "The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever". Part 1 was released on April 29, 2015 containing 9 tracks. This album debuted on the Billboard World's Ablum Charts at number 2 and had six songs on the Billboard World Digital Songs Chart. Part 2 was released on November 30, 2015 with 9 tracks. This album was on the Billboard World Digital Songs Charts as well with 8 tracks from the album on it. The album also peaked at number 1 on the Billboards Worlds Albums and continued to chart for 22 more weeks. The last album, Young Forever, contains all the songs from The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Part 1 and 2 plus 3 new singles and several new remixes. It was released on May 2, 2016. It debuted number 2 on the Billboard Worlds Album. Their 3 lead singles lined up on the top 3 ranks on the Billboard World Digital Songs which has never been done by a Korean act.
