WINGS
About the Album
WINGS was released on Ocotber 10, 2016 by Big Hit Entertainment. It includes 15 tracks with 7 solo tracks by each member. According to Gaon Charts, this album was the best selling album in Korea in 2016. It was then repackages with the album title "You Never Walk Alone" on February 13, 2017. It includes all the 15 tracks from "Wings" plus 3 more singles.

Tour
The 2017 BTS Live Trilogy Episode III: The Wings Tour dates were from February 18,2017 to December 10,2017. 40 concerts were held with 30 in Asia, 5 in North America, 4 in South America and 1 in Oceania.
